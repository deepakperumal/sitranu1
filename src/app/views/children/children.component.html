Gayathri Senthilkumar

Gayathri Senthilkumar is currently pursuing her 9th standard. She comes from a remote village near Pennagaram in Dharmapuri district and is supported by a single mother and a younger brother. Her mother works as an assistant in a local clinic and her family has a monthly income of only Rs. 4000 (~50 Euros).

She studied in Tamil medium till 5th standard, then moved to English medium in a government high school. Since June 2017, from her 8th standard, she is sponsored by Sitranu Foundation.

She aspires to be an IAS officer and we truly believe she will succeed.
